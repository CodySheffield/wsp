Samsung Galaxy NX

The Samsung Galaxy NX Mirrorless Digital Camera features a 20.3 megapixel APS-C CMOS sensor and DRIMe IV image processor to produce high resolution still imagery and full HD 1080p video at 30 fps with notable low-light sensitivity to ISO 25600. Samsung's interchangeable lens system allows this mirrorless camera to be paired with any lens in the NX lineup.
